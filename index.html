How It Works

The ESP8266 collects data from the ultrasonic sensor and TDS meter, controls the relay to manage the pump, and provides alerts through the LED and buzzer. The system is monitored and controlled remotely using the Blynk app.

Connection Details

The Ultrasonic Sensor's Trig pin is connected to D8, and the Echo pin to D7 on the ESP8266. The TDS Meter's analog output is connected to A0 on the ESP8266. The Relay, LED, and Buzzer are connected to D2, D3, and D4 respectively.
